Common Exercises

The Following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise rountine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quicky, and then jump from your squated position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a pushup position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain Climber are good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in the pushup position, then bring your one knee up to your chest then switch as quickly you can (as though you are climbing a mountain).
